The reflective facades of urban neighborhoods reflect the image of the city and help to reinforce the self-image of the latter in the eyes of the viewer. But what happens when the relationship between mirror and reflection becomes unbalanced? What happens to the self-image when the image is distorted in an infinite number of mirrors and nothing remains of the being but an empty self-reference to the reflecting objects?
