3 Lasers, each one carries a sstv signal of a different galactic picture at different wavelengths (colors), are stretched like a net (with mirrows) through a corridor. At the start of their lightspeed journey they inherit their own interpretation of their part of the galaxy; At the end, where all 3 lasers meet at the exact same point, interpretation, meaning, sound and stories mix up to a new sonification and visual interpretation.
