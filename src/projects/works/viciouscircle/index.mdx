The synthesis of a sphere in rotating unbalance and of fixed boundaries make it possible to contemplate two seperate systems as one. The orb reacts to the paint that was previously applied according to Piet Mondrians technique for Composition with Lines (1971), leading to an autonomous feedback loop of movement. The person who set the systems in motion looses all influence over the orb’s ever-changing movements. Gathering up coats of paint, the sphere develops new, unforeseen motion sequences.
