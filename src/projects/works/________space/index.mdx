**\_\_\_\_**space is a side specific video performance which was shown in a last guerilla exhibition before the closing of the Generali Foundation in Vienna. Two performers are outlining the dimensions of chosen works from the Generali Foundation's collection for the last time in this building. At the same time the other artists of the exhibition are setting up their works. This video is running in a live-set up with a camera and a monitor in the exact same angle how it was recorded.
